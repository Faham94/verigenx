A_002 critical RESET_VIOLATION
Timestamp: 0

What Happened

During active reset (rstn is low), txdata asserted high values instead of remaining zero.

Why

The driver failed to gate transmission registers when the active-low reset was asserted.

Suggested Fix

Modify the RTL always block to reset rxdata and txdata registers to 0 on negative edge of rstn.

Specification Reference
